Tic-Tac-Toe Multiplayer

Challenge friends online with room-based real-time matches.

How to Play

  • Create a room or join with a room code.
  • Invite your friend to the same room.
  • Take turns placing X and O.
  • First player to get 3 in a row wins.
  • Use in-game chat while playing.

Requirements

  • Internet connection is required.
  • Two real players are required (no AI mode).

Troubleshooting

Can’t connect:

  • Check internet on both devices.
  • Confirm both players use the exact same room code.
  • Try creating a new room code.

Moves not syncing:

  • Both players should have stable connectivity.
  • Close and reopen the app, then rejoin.
