Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

Vylos AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Tic-Tac-Toe Multiplayer app.

Information We Collect

Player Name: We store the display name you choose when joining a game. This is used to identify you to other players in the same room.

Chat Messages: Messages sent during gameplay are transmitted in real-time to other players in your room. We do not permanently store chat messages.

Room IDs: We track active game rooms to facilitate multiplayer connections.

How We Use Information

  • To enable real-time multiplayer gameplay
  • To display player names during matches
  • To facilitate in-game chat between players
  • To maintain game state and scores

Data Storage

Game data is stored temporarily during active gameplay sessions. Room data and player information are automatically cleared when all players leave a room.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Render: For backend game server hosting
  • Socket.io: For real-time communication
  • Vercel: For web hosting and deployment

Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including input validation and rate limiting on our servers.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Choose any player name (no account required)
  • Leave a game room at any time
  • Contact us with privacy questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
