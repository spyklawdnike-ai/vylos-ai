Aarambh Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

Aarambh ("we", "our", "us") is a spiritual reading companion focused on daily Hindu verses, reminders, and progress tracking. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Usage Analytics: app events such as screen interactions and feature usage.
  • Diagnostics: crash and performance diagnostics to improve stability.
  • Reminder Preferences: local reminder settings (such as selected time/category) stored on your device.

We do not require account sign-in for core app usage.

How We Use Data

  • To understand app usage and improve user experience.
  • To detect, investigate, and fix crashes or reliability issues.
  • To support reminder and streak-related app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Aarambh uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) for usage analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) for crash diagnostics and stability monitoring.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers listed above solely for analytics and diagnostics purposes.

Data Retention

Diagnostic and analytics data retention is managed by the respective third-party platform policies. Local app preferences remain on your device unless removed by uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

Aarambh is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications from your device settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and uninstall it.
