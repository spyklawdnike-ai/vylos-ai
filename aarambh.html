Aarambh

Your daily spiritual companion for mindful verse reading and consistency.

What You Can Do

  • Read curated daily spiritual verses in a distraction-free player.
  • Enable auto-scroll and tune reading speed/font for comfort.
  • Set daily reminders for selected paths.
  • Track streaks, progress history, and mindful minutes.
  • Share your progress cards with friends and family.

Quick Start

  • Open the app and choose a verse category from Home.
  • Start reading in Player mode.
  • Visit Settings to configure reminders.
  • Open Profile to view streaks and history.

Troubleshooting

Content not loading:

  • Check your internet connection and reopen the app.
  • Pull to refresh on Home screen.

Reminder not firing:

  • Ensure Notifications are allowed in iOS Settings.
  • Re-save reminder time in app Settings.

Share action failed:

  • Try again after reopening the app.
  • Ensure destination app permissions are available.
